About Study Buddy

Helping students learn together

Study Buddy is a student-created platform designed to help classmates support each other in their learning. It allows students to book study sessions and connect with volunteers who are willing to help.

Our goal is to make studying more collaborative, easier, and more enjoyable for everyone. By working together, students can build confidence, improve their understanding, and succeed academically.

Our Purpose

Many students find it difficult to study alone or feel unsure asking for help. Study Buddy creates a supportive environment where students can learn together and help each other grow.

How It Works

Students can book study sessions during break and lunch times, while volunteers sign up based on their availability. This makes it easy to connect and collaborate in a simple and effective way.

The Impact

Study Buddy builds a strong learning community, reduces stress, and encourages teamwork. It makes studying more enjoyable and helps students gain confidence in their learning journey.
